About Us

At Door to Dorm, we understand that moving can be stressful, especially for college students. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing a seamless, stress-free experience from start to finish.

Our team is committed to offering high-quality, reliable moving services tailored to students’ unique needs. We focus on:

  • Exceptional customer service
  • Safe and reliable moving practices
  • Convenient scheduling to fit your academic calendar

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Question: What services do you provide?

    Answer: We specialize in helping college students move out of their dorms at the end of the year. We pick up your belongings from your dorm (specified in the moving form) and deliver them to an address you provide later. The process is seamless and stress-free.

  • Question: How do I schedule my move?

    Answer: You fill out a moving form on our website, providing the dorm location, delivery address, and the number of boxes you need. You'll also select a 4-hour time slot for both pickup and delivery.

  • Question: How much does it cost?

    Answer: Each box costs $50. If you act before March 1st, you'll receive a 20% discount on each box. Additional charges apply for items like TVs and mini-fridges.

  • Question: What if I have a mini-fridge or large items?

    Answer: You can either pay an additional charge for us to transport the item or choose to donate your mini-fridge. Either way, ensure these items are clearly labeled using the labels we provide.

  • Question: How do I label my belongings?

    Answer: We send labels along with the boxes you request. Use these labels to clearly mark all your items, including additional items like mini-fridges or TVs, to ensure accurate handling and delivery.
